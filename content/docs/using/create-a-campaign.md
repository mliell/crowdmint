# Create a Campaign

Learn how to launch your own campaign on CrowdMint and start raising funds for your project.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:

- A Web3 wallet connected and funded with USDC on ARC blockchain
- Your campaign details ready (title, description, goals)
- Campaign visuals (optional but recommended)

## Campaign Setup

When creating a campaign, you'll need to provide:

- **Title:** A clear, engaging name (max 100 characters)
- **Description:** What your project is and why it matters, including milestones.
- **Goal Amount:** Target funding in USDC
- **Deadline**: When your campaign ends (30-90 days recommended)
- **Category**: Choose the most relevant one
