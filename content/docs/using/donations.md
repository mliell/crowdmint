# Backing Campaigns

Support projects you believe in by donating USDC directly through your Web3 wallet.

## How to Back a Campaign

### 1. Browse Campaigns

Explore active campaigns on the platform. Use filters or search to find causes that interest you.

### 2. Review Details

Before donating, check:

- Campaign goals and milestones
- Creator profile
- Funding progress
- Deadline and timeline

### 3. Make a Donation

Click "Donate", enter the amount in USDC, and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

- **Minimum:** No minimum (unless the creator sets a minimum)
- **Maximum:** No limit
